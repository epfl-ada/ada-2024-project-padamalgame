Red Work : Rubedo.

* The Red Work, or Rubedo, is the final stage of alchemy, representing completion and perfection. This phase is characterized by the material achieving a state of wholeness and unity, often symbolized by the creation of the Philosopher's Stone. The Red Work signifies the culmination of the alchemical journey, where the purified and enlightened substance attains its highest potential. It embodies a harmonious balance and the realization of the alchemist's ultimate goal.
