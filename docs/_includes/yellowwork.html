Yellow Work : Citrinitas.

* The Yellow Work, or Citrinitas, signifies the dawning of a new awareness and the integration of knowledge. This stage is associated with the color yellow or gold, symbolizing the rising sun and intellectual illumination. In this phase, the purified matter from the White Work begins to undergo a process of rejuvenation and revitalization. It marks the transition from the raw material to a more refined state, embodying creativity and the synthesis of understanding.
